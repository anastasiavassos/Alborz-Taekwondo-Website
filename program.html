The Program

Alborz Taekwondo is dedicated to developing accomplished martial artists. Our program reflects the philosophy, that given discipline and commitment, anyone can learn Taekwondo.

There are six belts within our program; white, yellow, green, blue, red and black. Students work through the program at their own pace as they develop the skills necessary to advance to the next level.

We have three class groups; children under 8 years of age, children 8 to 13 years of age and adults. Each class has a similar structure and begins with a 15-20 minute warm up and stretching. The remainder of the class is devoted to developing the skills necessary to advance through the belt system.

Each belt level has a set of required skills which include various kicks, punches, blocks, poomsae (patterns), and Taekwondo history and terminology. If you are interested, you can click on the buttons below to see the belt requirements.

YELLOW BELT

This program is designed to introduce the student to the basics of Taekwondo and prepare them mentally and physically for the upcoming intermediate techniques.

Skills Learned

• focus on developing goal setting
• prepare the body by developing the needed flexibility and strength
• develop confidence, self-control and non-violent confrontation management
• learn basic blocking and striking with hands and arms
• learn basic kicking techniques
• 2 poomsae (patterns)

GREEN BELT

Students are introduced to intermediate level skills and are encouranged to continually develop their newly acquired skills.

Skills Learned:

• Continue to develop sense of kindness and courtesy towards self and others
• skills from earlier belt are reviewed
• various new intermediate hands techinques
• learn new intermediate kicking techniques
• 2 additional poomsae (patterns)

Blue Belt

Working at this level indicates that the student is almost at the half-way mark on their journey to black belt.

Skills Learned:

• Develop a strong sense of commitment
• Encourange respect for the family and society's rules
• Continue to practice skills from earlier belts
• Introduced to basic Taekwondo history and philosophy
• Learn intermediate-advanced hands and kicking techniques
• Introduced to use of martial arts weapons (adults only)
• 2 additional poomsae (patterns)
• Beginner sparring skills

Red Belt

At this point, students move onto more advanced skills and techniques which require substantial patience and personal discipline.

Skills Learned:

• Develop a sense of patience with themselves and others
• Introduced to additional Taekwondo history and philosophy
• Continue to practice skills from earlier belts
• Advanced hands and kicking techniques
• Intermediate connecting hand and foot work
• Continued martial arts weapons training (adults only)
• 2 additional poomsae (patterns)
• Intermediate sparring skills

Black Belt

This level stresses the significance of what it will mean to obtain a black belt. The discipline, perseverence and dedication needed to acheive this last belt and start the next phase of the journey into developing oneself as a true Martial Artist.

Skills Learned:

• An indepth review of Taekwondo history and philosophy
• Continue to practice skills from earlier belts
• Advanced hands and kicking techniques with combinations
• Advanced connecting hands and footwork
• Adults continue to develop martial arts weapons training
• 2 additional poomsae (patterns)
• Advanced sparring skills
• Preparation for black belt testing
